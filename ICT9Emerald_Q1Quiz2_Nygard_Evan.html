Ratatouille

Ratatouille is a Pixar film released in 2007. The story is about a rat who dreams of being a chef. Separated from his family, the rat, Remy, ends up in Paris, befriending an incompetent young man. The man, Linguini, has no ability to cook, so Remy controls him by pulling his hair. Their strategy encounters many problems, as the culinary industry despises rats and the boss of their restaurant wants full control.
